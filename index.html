What is Wolof Academy?

The Wolof Academy is a project launched in August 2020 to provide high-quality resources to anyone willing to learn Wolof online. We noticed how tedious it was to find intuitive Wolof courses on the internet so we came up with this solution to solve the problem. We’re continuously working on the project to provide our community with the best learning experience.

Follow us on Instagram

Our Instagram page is the place where we are currently the most active. We share valuable content at least twice a week and regularly interact with our community to gather their feedback in order to provide them with the best content.

We also organize interactive quizzes through which you can strengthen your knowledge and learn new concepts while having fun.

What are you waiting for? Go follow us if you don’t want to miss out on anything!

Subscribe to our email list and get notified of any update such as new quizzes, new e-books, new videos, and so on. You can also benefit from early access to all our future releases.
